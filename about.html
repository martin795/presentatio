ABOUT


Dr. (Mrs.) Alimatu - Saadia Yussiff is a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology, University of Cape Coast. She has taught relevant courses in the department, and assumed responsibilities of regular university lecturer including examination officer, registration officer and academic advisor. She is a dedicated, professional and accomplished lecturer with knowledge of teaching computer science and Information Technology courses.

Her research Activities Include:


  • E-learning Related Topics
  • Intelligent tutoring systems
  • Mobile and Ubiquitous Systems
  • Transcript analysis of users collaborative learning experience
  • Internet of things (IoTs)
  • Science Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Education and System Development
  • Semantic Web Applications

She is a member of the following professional bodies:

  • University Teachers Association of Ghana (UTAG) 2007 – Date
  • Ghana Science Association (GSA) 2009 – Date
    • (Treasurer to GSA, September 2019 - Date)
  • Information Technologist Association of Ghana (ITAG)
  • Internet Society Ghana (ISOC)
  • African Network Operators Group (AFNOG)
  • Women in Information Technology (WoTI)
  • African Women in Computing (Afchix)
  • Women TechMakers
  • Women in Science Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), UCC
